# Overview

Student ID: `C24053582`

Aura Health is a preventative healthcare journaling application that tracks symptoms and correlates them with real-time environmental data. Users log symptoms from a predefined catalogue or as custom entries. On save, device location services fetch live weather, air quality and barometric pressure from Open-Meteo and bind the snapshot to the log. AI analysis outlines any correlations across recent entries, and pushes a notification when the result is ready. Reports can be exported as a compressed PDF file to share with their doctor for their next appointment. 

Those with environmentally sensitive conditions such as migraines, asthma or hayfever currently have no way to correlate their symptoms to triggers like pollution, temperature and humidity, without manually cross referencing weather data. Aura Health assists with this by capturing the environmental data automatically so the conditions are recorded live rather than reconstructed afterwards.

The app is focused on those who want to take a preventative approach to healthcare as well as anyone managing an environmentally sensitive condition. By bridging symptoms with environmental data and using AI to analyse this data, users can make informed lifestyle changes and bring empirically grounded context to medical appointments.
